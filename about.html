Who We Are

TAP (The Artist Playground) is a mobile-first creative media company. We're based in San Diego, powered by culture and community, and designed to help individuals and organizations broadcast their stories to the world.

Founded by Tyrone and Tay, TAP is more than production—we’re cultural curators. Our work spans live streaming events, podcast creation, and artist mentorship. We believe in amplifying authentic voices and breaking down barriers to high-quality media.
