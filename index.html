About TAP

TAP is a creative media production company founded by Tyrone and Tay in San Diego. Our mission is to help artists, entrepreneurs, and communities share their stories through mobile live-streaming, podcasting, and artist development. We bring studio-quality production to your doorstep.

Our Services

🎥 Live Event Streaming

Weddings, funerals, concerts, and community events streamed live.

🎙 Podcast Production

Audio & video recording, editing, and multi-platform streaming.

🎨 Artist Development

Coaching and content tools to support emerging creatives.

📷 360° Virtual Tours

Immersive tours for real estate, venues, community spaces, and more.

Why Choose TAP

  • Mobile Studio: We bring the gear, crew, and expertise anywhere.
  • Multi-Camera: Engage your audience with dynamic angles & broadcast-grade audio.
  • Fast Turnaround: Reliable setup, streaming, and mailed recordings.
  • Community Focus: We invest in underserved stories and voices.
